AscendAI

2023

An Android app for analysing climbing and bouldering videos. Uses a locally running pose estimation to calculate and visualize various metrics like movement speed, jerk, geometric entropy, or joint-angles. Enables the user to compare multiple attempts to each other.

  • AscendAI on Google Play

CryptoTweet

2021

Amid the crypto craze, I ran a tool that purchased cryptocurrencies when key personalities tweeted specific keywords. These tweets typically caused brief, unpredictable spikes, prompting the tool to buy and sell. It was a unique way to explore social media's impact on crypto trading.

  • https://github.com/JonasDeu/CryptoTweet
